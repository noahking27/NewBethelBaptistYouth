Lenwood V. Long, Jr.

Youth Pastor

Lenwood V. Long, Jr affectionately called “Rev. Wood” has been in ministry with New Bethel for 6 years under the tutelage of his father Pastor Lenwood V. Long Sr. Rev. Wood has led or co-led ministries such as Young Adults, Community Events & A/V. Wood is passionate about encouraging kids/people to believe in themselves beyond their potential, and on offering an encouraging word to inspire one’s day.

He is married to the amazing Jemeaka C. Long (Co-Leader COLA) with one child Skyler Long. Together Wood & Jemeaka work to develop leaders of tomorrow, while providing spiritual guidance to overcame any obstacles that children are confronted with today.
