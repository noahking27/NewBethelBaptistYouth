New Bethel Baptist Church Rolesville Youth Group

Mission

The mission of the NBBC Youth Ministry is to establish a foundation for building Christian character, morals, values, and behaviors in young people by using creative and innovative methods to excite, inspire, capture, and ignite young people for Jesus.

Services

Services are held 1st, 3rd, and 4th Sunday’s. ** 2nd Sunday’s are family ** Services begin at 10am.

Calendar –Events for the year inclusive of practices, trips, & leadership events

Community Events/Programs – Need to feed, community clean up, habitat for humanity, & other community driven events.

Volunteers

Volunteers – We welcome volunteers that have a commitment and passion to work with children to see their development into amazing leaders never forgetting the Word of God.
